Historic Racing Season

Motorsport has a rich history that dates back to the origins of the motor car. There are many events including displays and even races, with these great old machines.

You will find cars of all ages and types, competing or just being displayed, across a wide range of events in Britain throughout the year. Look below to see any you may like to attend.

UK Historic & Classic Motorsport Events

Race Retro

See great cars on display before the season starts in late February. With a small hillclimb at Stoneleigh Park in Coventry, see cars both old and new. You will also find many great displays.

Visit Web Page

Donington Historic Festival

Donington Park is one of the oldest cirucits in the country, hosting the British Grand Prix in the 1930's. What a great place to see some of the classic cars on display, and in action on the grand prix circuit.

Visit Web Page

Silverstone Classic

The Silverstone Classic takes place over three days in July every year. This is Silverstone's second busiest meeting of the year, after the Grand Prix. There are hundreds of cars, from different disciplines in action!

Visit Web Page

Classic TT

A few weeks after the official Isle of Man TT, the classic TT gets underway. See bikes from the past, once again take to the famed 37 mile circuit. A more relaxed atmosphere than the main event, but still a supurb event.

Visit Web Page

Oulton Park Gold Cup

Oulton Park in Cheshire plays host to this relaxed historic event. See touring cars, endurance racers, formula 3 and many other dispciplines race. There is plenty of off track action too!

Visit Web Page

Goodwood Revival

Every September, the world of motorsport returns to Goodwood in Sussex to the Revival meeting. Large crowds gather to watch classic cars racing, as well as displays. Period dress is also encouraged!
