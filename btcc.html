British Touring Car Championship

Founded in 1958, the British Touring Car Championship is one of Britain's oldest championships, and remains one of its most popular.

Cars are based on the common road going vehicles you will find on the general streets. The BTCC has a huge following, and you'll find large crowds at the events, which are held in all corners of the country.
