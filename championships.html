Championships

From two and four wheels, you will find many motorsports series in Britain, catering for a wide range of motorsports fans. Click through the list below to find a series that may interest you.

Touring Cars

Founded in 1958, the British Touring Car Championship is one of Britain's oldest championships, and remains one of its most popular.

Rallycross

British GT

The British GT Chappionship is the UK's highest level of sports car racing series. Teams and drivers from this series will often also take part in the Le Mans 24 Hours.

Superbikes

Dating back to 1988, the British Superbike Championship is the premier bike racing championship in Britain.

Rallying

The British Rally Championship had not been running since 2015, but is back for this year. Previously it produced champions that went on to compete in the WRC.
