British GT Championship

The British GT Chappionship is the UK's highest level of sports car racing series. Teams and drivers from this series will often also take part in the Le Mans 24 Hours.

The cars are modified versions of street going sportscars, and many of the top manufacturers are involved, including Ferrari, BMW, Aston Martin and Porsche. These also race on the major circuits in all parts of the UK.
