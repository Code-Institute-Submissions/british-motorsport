British Superbike Championship

Dating back to 1988, the British Superbike Championship is the premier championship on two wheels in Britain.

Superbikes are derived from the road going versions of the motorcycles, and current manufacturers competing include BMW, Ducati and Honda. Visiting circuits all over the country you are sure to find a race to visit near you.
