Events

Beyond the National Championships, there are multiple different events to visit. Scroll below and have a look through them. With thousands of possible events to visit, you'll find them all over the country. Here are some of the most popular!

2018 UK Motorsport Events

Autosport International

A great way to start the motorsport year. Held in January annually at the NEC in Birmingham, Autosport International is the largest motorsport show in the world! It takes place over four days, two for trade, and two four the public.

Visit Web Page

Race Retro

See great cars on display before the season starts in late February. With a small hillclimb at Stoneleigh Park in Coventry, see cars both old and new. You will also find many great displays.

Visit Web Page

London Classic Car Show

Displays of great classic cars. From Ford, Ferrari, Jaguar, and any other manufacturers you care to think of. It takes place every February. Held at the Excel centre, you'll find it's easy to get to!

Visit Web Page

Isle of Man TT

Possibly the most dangerous sporting event on the planet! Held since 1907, the Isle of Man TT is held over two weeks in June. Bikes and sidecars take part in races through winding country roads and towns at speeds of up to 200mph!

Visit Web Page

Goodwood Festival of Speed

Held late June or early July at Goodwood House in Sussex, watch a wide range of cars be driven up the hillclimb. From Formula One, Nascar and classic racers. This is one of world motorsports most popular annual events.

Visit Web Page

Goodwood Revival

Every September, the world of motorsport returns to Goodwood in Sussex to the Revival meeting. Large crowds gather to watch classic cars racing, as well as displays. Period dress is also encouraged!

Visit Web Page

Festival of Porsche

Porsche organised special event at Brands Hatch. See a vast array of cars from the famed German manufacturer. This includes a lap record attempt by the 2017 Le Mans 24hr winner, the Porsche 919!

Visit Web Page

Formula Ford Festival

Watch Formula Ford Cars do battle in this exciting, multi race event. It takes place every year at Brands Hatch and has a vast list of previous winners. A very good price with a relaxed atmospher and easy access.

Visit Web Page

Walter Hayes Trophy

One of the largest grids assembled for a race meeting, the Walter Hayes is a great way to close the national racing calendar. With Formula Ford, close racing is guaranteed!

Visit Web Page
