British Motorsport

In Britain, motorsport is very popular, playing host to multiple world championship events in series including Formula 1, World Endurance Championship and MotoGP.

But you will also find many National Championships. Have a look through this site to see which you may be interested in! Find out latest events and schedules.

Championships

From two and four wheels, you will find many motorsports series in Britain, catering for a wide range of motorsports fans. Click through the list below to find a series that may interst you!

British Touring Car Championship

Founded in 1958, the British Touring Car Championship is one of Britain's oldest championships, and remains one of its most popular.

British Rallycross Championship

With a history in Britain that dates back to 1967, the British Rallycross championship is a very popular series.

British GT Championship

The British GT Chappionship is the UK's highest level of sports car racing series. Teams and drivers from this series will often also take part in the Le Mans 24 Hours.

British Superbike Championship

Dating back to 1988, the British Superbike Championship is the premier bike racing championship in Britain.

British Rally Championship

The British Rally Championship has not been running since 2015, but has previously had many champions that have gone on to compete in the WRC.

Historic Racing Season

Motorsport has a rich history that dates back to the origins of the motor car. There are many events including displays and even races, with these great old machines.

Events and Circuits

Apart from the championships, you will find many more events, including rallys, displays and races to attend. Britain is one of the best countries in the world for circuits, and there is sure to be a local circuit to you.
