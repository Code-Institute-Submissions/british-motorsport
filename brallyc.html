British Rally Championship

The British Rally Championship has previously had many champions that have gone on to compete in the WRC. These include some of Britains top drivers including Colin McRae and Richard Burns.

The competition is held on stages around a main location. The surfaces can vary, from tarmac, gravel and even snow. The fastest driver over these stages is declared the winner.
